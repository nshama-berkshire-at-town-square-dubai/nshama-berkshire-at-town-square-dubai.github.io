Nshama Berkshire at Town Square Dubai Payment Plan

The Nshama Berkshire Park payment plan is designed to make homeownership and investment opportunities more accessible and convenient. With a 50/50 payment structure, buyers have the flexibility to secure their future homes with ease.

During Construction (50%): Buyers are required to pay 50% of the total property value in phases during the construction period. This allows ample time to spread out payments, making it more manageable without the pressure of a lump-sum upfront cost.

On Handover (50%): The remaining 50% of the payment is due upon the project's handover in Q2 2026. This allows homeowners to pay the balance when they receive the keys to their new home, ensuring a smooth and financially viable process.
